Textiles of Bihar

Bihar's traditional textile heritage includes the unique and vibrant Chingari work, a form of hand embroidery that reflects cultural stories and motifs.

Chingari Embroidery

Chingari is a traditional hand-embroidery art native to Bihar, featuring flame-like patterns that resemble sparks, hence the name "Chingari" which means "spark" in Hindi.

Technique: Intricate needlework done using colorful threads, often on cotton or silk fabrics. Patterns include motifs inspired by nature, festivals, and village life.

Artisan Work: Practiced mostly by rural women, this form of art serves as a livelihood source and a medium for storytelling.

Recognition: Though not GI tagged yet, Chingari embroidery is gaining popularity for its uniqueness and cultural value.
